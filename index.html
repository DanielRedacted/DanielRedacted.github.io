redact

/rĭ-dăkt′/

transitive verb

  1. To draw up or frame (a proclamation, for example).
  2. To make ready for publication; edit or revise.
  3. To delete or remove (private or sensitive information) from a document in preparation for publication.

example: █ ████ ███!



note: Redaction is used by Governments and Legal entities, Companies, Journalists, and Medical Institutions to protect sensitive and confidential information, personal data, and identities. You should too.
